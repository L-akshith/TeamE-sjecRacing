About eSJEC Racing

SYS.01 // POWERTRAIN

Electric Powertrain

Pioneering electric ATV technology with custom-designed powertrains pushing the boundaries of performance and efficiency.

SYS.02 // FABRICATION

Built From Scratch

Every component engineered and fabricated by students — from roll cage design to suspension geometry and electronics.

SYS.03 // TELEMETRY

Competition Ready

Competing in BAJA SAE India and ATVC national championships, consistently ranking among India's top teams.

SYS.04 // INTEGRATION

Multidisciplinary

Students from Mechanical, E&E, ECE, and CS departments collaborating to create engineering excellence.

Established in March 2019 under the SAEINDIA SJEC Collegiate Club, Team eSJEC Racing is a student-led initiative from St. Joseph Engineering College, Mangalore. We design, fabricate, and race electric All-Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) that compete at the national level. Our vehicle, the Beetle series, represents generations of innovation and relentless engineering pursuit.
